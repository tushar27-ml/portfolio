UNITED NATIONS YOUTH INDIA

Description

I am the Deputy Secretary General with the United Nations Youth India, which is India's first U.N. Youth organization officially registered under the UN75. I was selected as one of the National Speakers at the National Dialogue sessions voicing my opinion about the climate crisis and a Member of the Jury at the National Level Ideathon devising feasible solutions to ensure uninterrupted education to underprivileged children amidst the covid-19 crisis.


-Spearheaded the UNYI Mission 75 officially registered under the UN75.
-Selected as the National Speaker at the National Dialogue sessions attended by 75+ youth leaders.
-Managed collaborations for the United Climate front attended by 30+ global and national climate activists.
-Selected as the Member of the Jury for the Youth Spark Ideathon to devise practical solutions to ensure uninterrupted education to children amidst the covid-19 pandemic.
